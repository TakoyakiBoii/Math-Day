About Us!

What is Amy's Learning Academy?

Here at Amy's Learning Academy, we strive for learners to have a fun experience learning math,
one of the hardest yet an essential part we need to learn in our lives. We want to encourage
everyone to be able to learn math at their own pace and have fun while doing it!

Mission:

We have noticed that there has been a surge of people struggling to comprehend even basic
mathematics, so we decided that we wanted to supply ample learning materials in order to
let learners learn at their own pace and really understand the fundamentals of math while
making it entertaining.

Vision:

Our vision perfectly aligns with SDG 4: Quality Education, in which we strive to
ensure inclusive and equitable quality education for all, we also wish to see that
the amount of people struggling in mathematics decrease.

We'll continue to supply learning materials and educate people as much as we can.
If you're new to this site please feel free to look around, and to those who have
lost hope when it comes to learning math. There is no such person too hopeless to
learn math, we'll keep trying as long as you do your part and make an effort to
learn as well!

What is SDG 4?

One of the 17 sustainable development goals, SDG 4 aims to give everyone inclusive and
equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

SDG 4 actually has 8 main targets, you can see them on this site here → SDG 4: Quality Education
