About Us!

What is Amy's Learning Academy?

Here at Amy's Learning Academy, we strive for learners to have a fun experience learning math,
one of the hardest yet an essential part we need to learn in our lives. We want to encourage
everyone to be able to learn math at their own pace and have fun while doing it!

Why did we make Amy's Learning Academy?

Research from The Organization for Economic Co-Operation and Development shows that 25% of
adults have a difficult time using math in their everyday life. The United Nations
Educational Scientific and Cultural Organization has found that around 387 million
kids finish school without even having the math skills they should have. Lastly, some
researchers found out that 3-7% of people have a learning disability called dyscalculia,
this learning disorder makes it difficult for people who have it to understand numbers and math.

The study above is the reason why we made Amy's Learning Academy, we want everyone to have
a positive experience learning math and numbers. Moreover, we want learners to have the
sufficient knowledge of math when they finish school, 3-4% of adults not being able to use
math in their lives is very alarming as math is a big part of day to day life. We'd like
everybody to have at least a basic understanding of math in order to avoid having them be
part of the 3-4% of those adults, and ultimately decrease that percentage altogether.

Mission:

We have noticed that there has been a surge of people struggling to comprehend even basic
mathematics, so we decided that we wanted to supply ample learning materials in order to
let learners learn at their own pace and really understand the fundamentals of math while
making it entertaining.

Vision:

Our vision perfectly aligns with SDG 4: Quality Education, in which we strive to
ensure inclusive and equitable quality education for all, we also wish to see that
the amount of people struggling in mathematics decrease.

We'll continue to supply learning materials and educate people as much as we can.
If you're new to this site please feel free to look around, and to those who have
lost hope when it comes to learning math. There is no such person too hopeless to
learn math, we'll keep trying as long as you do your part and make an effort to
learn as well!

What is SDG 4?

One of the 17 sustainable development goals, SDG 4 aims to give everyone inclusive and
equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

SDG 4 actually has 8 main targets, you can see them on

Inspiration

The main vision for Amy's Learning Academy is that its supposed to look like
an edutainment website and a flash game from the 2000's, We also took inspiration
from a horror game named "Andy's Apple Farm" though the mascot isn't actually inspired
